Quesadilla

Ingredients:

  • Any Number of Tortillas You Want
  • Cooked Chicken
  • Mushrooms
  • Any Shredded Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Chicken.
  2. Cook the Mushrooms
  3. Add Cheese,Mushrooms,Chicken, and Put Them Together with the Tortilla.
  4. Grab a Skillet and Butter the Pan.
  5. Put the Tortilla on and Fry Till Golden Brown.

Drinks:

  • Water
  • Strawberry Smoothie
  • Sprite
  • Coke
